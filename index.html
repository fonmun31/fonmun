About the event

Fonsorbes Model United Nations (FONMUN) is a high school MUN conference with about 100 participants, organised by students from the Lycée Clemence Royer in Fonsorbes, France. The aim of these UN simulations is to introduce participants to international negotiations, but also to develop their English and oral skills in a smart and joyful way. FONMUN is a large-scale project with a big impact. In 2025, we are very pleased to set up 4 committees for FONMUN III :

  • United Nations Environmental Program or UNEP
  • United Nations Security Council or UNSC
  • Human Rights Committee or HRC
  • European Economic and Social Council or ECOSOC

Engage in riveting debates, forge alliances, and navigate the complexities of international relations. With the simulation, we try to imagine the shapes of a better society, always with diplomacy, debates, and respect. Experience diplomacy and intellectual exchanges at FONMUN, offered by our dedicated and local club. Immerse yourself in a dynamic gathering of passionate delegates, where FONMUN sets the stage for a transformative experience. We believe that, through FONMUN, we'll develop our potential and inspire positive changes. As Janusz Mathéo said in 2024: " Together we can achieve what may seem impossible "

FONMUN was originally created by enthusiastic students and professors in 2023. Due to their hard work and the time they spend, we are today able to renew this outstanding experience. These students were partly motivated by TOUMUN , another simulation in Southern France, earlier in the year. FONMUN represents a local and student initiative, that has been built over the years with the help of a lot of people who gave tham all to the club to create this beautiful thing that is the MUN in Fonsorbes.

Latest News


During the last two weeks, the resolution and backgrounds paper were written by the chairs and delegations of each committees, they'll be soon uploaded onto the website. FONMUN is coming in less than 2 weeks and evcerything is almost ready.


From January 29th to 31st, a group of 21 brave student went to our biggest conference of the year: the TOUMUN, in Tournefeuille, France. 20 went as delegates in differents committees such as DISEC (Disarmement), UNEP (Environnement), ECOSOC (Economic and Social), HRC (Human Rights), WHO (Health) or the SAF (Speial African Forum) and our Technical coordinator Mathis Nguyen Mory went as a chair in the DISEC committee and helped the organization of the conference. Some of our delegates were really good in debate and elocution as Hugo Andriansalama, Clement Mernier and Simon Kroger succeded to get the honorable delegate of their committees award. The end of the conference was a real hearthbreak as the most part of us were about to graduate and it was their last TOUMUN experience. Tears and hugs were present when the moments for the goodbyes came. Pictures are available on the instagram and on the highschool website.


On Wednesday, 16th October, the newcomers went to the workshop of PIBMUN in Pibrac, France. Accompagnated with some of the most experienced, they exerced the new techniques they just learned and prepared themselves for bigger conferences by acquiring new skills and experience.
